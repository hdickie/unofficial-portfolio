Text Analysis & Infographics of H. P. Lovecraft's Fiction

H. P. Lovecraft is my favorite fiction author. I have memorized several of his short stories and when I'm feeling some poetic motivation, I will usually go for his cosmic horror style. I love his imagery and the psychological bend on the horror of his stories. Here is a great sentence from "Celephais":

"The abyss was now a seething chaos of roseate and cerulean splendour, and invisible voices sang exultantly as the knightly entourage plunged over the edge and floated gracefully down past glittering clouds and silvery coruscations."

I love it! I had a couple goals for this project: to explore the relationship between his fiction works, to absorb some of his style of imagery via word choice and combinations of descriptive words, and to generate new text in a Lovecraftian style.

Others have worked on this project before: the dataset is available on Kaggle (though I scraped it from a site dedicated to him). A Lovecraftian text generator is a project I found on Kaggle. These are not new. I googled around for Lovecraft infographics on his texts and didn't find any. I believe the infographics I (plan to) create will be some of the first. I also bring in supplemental data to aid in the analysis. At this point just a dictionary api to help identify phrases.
